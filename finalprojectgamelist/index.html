Submitting Your Game

Step 1

Make sure you pushed your game to github. Then login to your GitHub account and navigate to your game's index.html file.

Step 2

Copy the url of that page

Step 3

Visit rawgit.com and paste the url in the indicated section.

Step 4

Press the indicated button to copy a url that is publicly accessible.

Step 5

Paste your new, publicly accessible url in the "Game's Url" field below, fill out the required fields and hit the submit button!
